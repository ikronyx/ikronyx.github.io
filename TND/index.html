Treats & Delights

We at Treats & Delights offer you the yummiest brownies which are not too cakey but fudgy and gooey at the same time. We do brownie cakes which the toppings could be customized as per the requirement of our customers. We also have a variety of unique toppings to bring you the finest flavor combinations. Simply, our goal is to provide the customers with an absolutely delicious assortment of brownies for gift giving and to make your loved ones feel valued when celebrating the special events in their lives.
We assure the freshness of our brownies and much care is taken in each stage of preparation. We do have a minimum order quantity and we are flexible enough to offer you with any quantity of brownies above the minimum order quantity which is five. Furthermore, we do cater to parties and other events. We know how tiring your day can be. So, go ahead. Grab a piece of brownie from Treats & Delights and enjoy the moment.
